Our Mission

We’re exploring how code and power shape access, opportunity, and bias online, then making that knowledge practical for first-year students through a simple, accessible site.

Intersectionality matters: People experience technology differently across race, gender, class, ability, and citizenship. Our site highlights those overlaps with clear resources and approachable examples.
